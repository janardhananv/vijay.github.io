Budgets and policy

An Expense Management tool made for Open Money (Fintech platform)

Existing Design

The existing system had straightforward expense submission by employees and approval by admin.

Add expense - The process is simple. Employee adds the expense, it goes for approval

Objectives & Goals

- Introduce Budget and policies for a better Expense tracking

- Streamline the Expense approval, submission process.

- Multi level approval flow

Challenges

Configuring multi-level approval without a team-structure.

Transition from Open Account balance to Spend limit

Inclusion of a rules and regulations for expenses

Integrating a entire tool into the part of the open platform

Educating the existing users and make smooth transition

Retaining user information of existing users

Product Users

SME Organisation Owners and employees

- Group expense which are similar for an easy approvals.
- Assign ‘delegates’ to reduce workload

Employee

- Better tracking of expenses.
